# Research Notes

## Task
Ethical Hacking Task 01 – Information Gathering & Reconnaissance

## Target

Website Name: OpenAI

Website URL: https://openai.com

## Reason for Choosing

OpenAI is a well-known public organization. Its website contains publicly available information that can be collected ethically through passive reconnaissance techniques.

## Passive Reconnaissance

Passive reconnaissance is the process of collecting publicly available information about a target without directly interacting with or attacking its systems.

## Information to Collect

- WHOIS Information
- DNS Records
- Website Technologies
- HTTP Security Headers
- Robots.txt
- Sitemap.xml

## Tools Used

- WHOIS Lookup
- DNS Lookup
- Wappalyzer
- SecurityHeaders.com

## Observation

Passive reconnaissance helps identify valuable information about a target while minimizing the risk of detection. It is an essential first step in penetration testing and ethical hacking.
