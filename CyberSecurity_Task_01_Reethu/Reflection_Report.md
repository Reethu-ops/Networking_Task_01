# Security Reflection

While performing this security audit, I identified several cybersecurity risks in my digital habits. Although I use strong passwords for most accounts, I noticed that some accounts did not have Multi-Factor Authentication enabled. This increases the risk of unauthorized access if a password is compromised.

I also realized the importance of keeping software and operating systems updated. Security updates help protect devices from newly discovered vulnerabilities and cyber threats. During the assessment, I verified that antivirus protection and firewall settings were enabled on my system, which provides an additional layer of security.

One improvement I plan to implement immediately is enabling MFA on all important accounts, including social media and email services. I will also review old accounts and update weak passwords using a stronger password policy. In addition, I will be more cautious when clicking links in emails and messages to avoid phishing attacks.

Cybersecurity is important in today's world because personal and professional activities depend heavily on digital systems. Cyber attacks can lead to data theft, financial loss, and privacy violations. By following good cybersecurity practices such as strong passwords, regular updates, and awareness of online threats, individuals can better protect themselves and their information. This task helped me understand that cybersecurity starts with personal responsibility and secure habits.
