# CIA Triad Research

## Confidentiality

### Definition
Confidentiality ensures that information is accessible only to authorized users.

### Importance
It protects sensitive data from unauthorized access.

### Example
Only students and faculty should access student records.

---

## Integrity

### Definition
Integrity ensures that data remains accurate and unchanged.

### Importance
It prevents unauthorized modification of information.

### Example
Exam results should not be altered without authorization.

---

## Availability

### Definition
Availability ensures systems and data are accessible whenever needed.

### Importance
Users must be able to access services without interruption.

### Example
The college website should remain accessible during admissions.

---
