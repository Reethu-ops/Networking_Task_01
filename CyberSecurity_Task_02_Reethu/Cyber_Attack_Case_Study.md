# WannaCry Ransomware Case Study

## What Happened?

WannaCry was a ransomware attack that spread globally in 2017. It encrypted files on infected computers and demanded payment to restore access.

## Which CIA Component Was Affected?

### Availability
Users could not access their files and systems.

### Integrity
Files were modified and encrypted.

## Impact

- Over 200,000 computers affected.
- Hospitals, businesses, and government organizations faced disruptions.
- Financial losses worldwide.

## Prevention

- Regular system updates.
- Security patches.
- Antivirus protection.
- Regular backups.
- Employee awareness training.
