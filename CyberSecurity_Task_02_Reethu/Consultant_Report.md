# Cybersecurity Improvement Report

## Top 5 Risks

1. Data Breaches
2. Phishing Attacks
3. Malware Infections
4. Weak Passwords
5. Unpatched Systems

## Top 5 Recommendations

1. Enable MFA
2. Regular Updates
3. Strong Password Policies
4. Employee Training
5. Regular Backups

## Expected Benefits

- Better protection of student and faculty data.
- Reduced risk of cyber attacks.
- Faster recovery from incidents.
- Improved system reliability.
- Stronger overall security posture.

## Conclusion

The college can significantly improve cybersecurity by implementing these recommendations. Regular monitoring, security awareness, and strong access controls will help protect critical information and systems.
